Who We Are?

Cornerstone Christian Academy was started by a group of parents and teachers who wanted to provide quality Christian education for their families and the surrounding community. In striving to accomplish this goal we have placed Christ in the center of our school as the Cornerstone upon which a strong education is built.

We Want You!

At Cornerstone our goal is to assist parents in helping their children to grow spiritually, mentally, socially, and physically. We strive to provide a comprehensive education with high academic standards embedded in Christian principles. Our teaching is based upon the Bible as God's Word, and the Lordship of Jesus Christ. This program is administered by WA State certified Christian teachers who are role models for your student.

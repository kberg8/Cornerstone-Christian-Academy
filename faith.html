Cornerstone Christian Faith & Values

Statement of Faith

In John 14:6 Jesus said, “I am the way, the truth, and the life: no man cometh unto the Father, but by me”. Cornerstone Christian Academy is committed to educating and training children in “The Way”, “The Truth”, and “The Life”.

The Way: Deuteronomy 6:7 and Proverbs 22:6 show that that the primary responsibility of education lies with the parents. Cornerstone aids parents and helps facilitate the process of each child learning the ways of the Lord.

The Truth: In John 8:31-32 Jesus spoke to His followers about continuing in His Word, being disciples, knowing the truth, and the truth setting them free. Cornerstone believes in disciplining children in the truths of the Bible, so that as they grow into men and women of God, they will in turn disciple others to follow Jesus. We also believe that one of education’s main goals is to search out truth. As educators, we will try to bring truth to subject areas and will encourage students to use Biblical truths to interpret what they see and hear.

The Life: Matthew 6:33 tells us that we are to seek first the Kingdom of God and all of life’s needs shall be added unto us. At Cornerstone we know that if we teach our students to seek the Kingdom of God first and foremost in their lives, that Jesus will be faithful to fulfill His promise to each individual.

Cornerstone Christian Academy's Mission Statement

We will provide a comprehensive education with high academic standards embedded in Christian principles. We will help children grow mentally, socially, physically, and spiritually. Our teaching is based upon the Bible as God’s Word, and the lordship of Jesus Christ. We have established the following goals to facilitate in accomplishing our mission.

  • To provide non-denominational, Biblical teaching that will teach the basic general Christian beliefs and principles listed in our Statement of Faith.
  • To provide teaching, encouragement, specific guidelines, as well as appropriate discipline, in order to build strong Christian character in each student.
  • To provide a strong academic program that satisfies state academic requirements and prepares young people to live successfully in this world. Students will be given a strong foundation in the basics of Bible, reading, language, literature, mathematics, science and history.
  • To provide upstanding, certified Christian teachers who will be role models for the students.

About

Cornerstone Christian Academy was started by a group of parents and teachers who wanted to provide quality Christian education for their families and the surrounding community. In striving to accomplish this goal we have placed Christ in the center of our school as the Cornerstone upon which a strong education is built.

About Cornerstone Christian Academy

At Cornerstone our goal is to assist parents in helping their children to grow spiritually, mentally, socially, and physically. We strive to provide a comprehensive education with high academic standards embedded in Christian principles. Our teaching is based upon the Bible as God's Word, and the Lordship of Jesus Christ. This program is administered by WA State certified Christian teachers who are role models for your student.

Cornerstone Academy is accredited through the The Northwest Accreditation Commission (NWAC) which is an accreditation division of AdvancED.

School Uniforms

All students enrolled in the Academy, through 6th grade are required to wear the following school uniform...

  • Navy or Khaki twill pants or shorts (girls may wear skirts no shorter than 1" above the knee)
  • Polo or button down shirts, short or longed sleeved, white or navy blue.
  • Plain sweatshirt with or without CCA logo, white or navy blue over a collared shirt.
  • Cardigan or Sweater vest, solid white or navy blue.
  • Shoes must have a non-marking sole and be a closed toe shoe with a back. No Flip Flops.

There are some limited exception to the school uniform policy including pre-determined "wear your own clothes (WYOC) days" and school spirit days. Modesty and good judgment are always required and students choosing not to participate in planned events are required to wear the assigned school uniform. Details about acceptable clothing is available in the student handbook.

Tuition & Fees

Tuition can be paid in 10 equal monthly payments.

  • Registration: $50.00 - All Grades
  • Supply Fee: $200.00 - K Through 6th Grade
  • Supply Fee: $250.00 - 7th & 8th Grade
  • Tuition: $4000.00 - K through 6th Grade
  • Tuition: $4275.00 - 7th & 8th Grade

Classes at Cornerstone Christian

Cornerstone Christian Academy (CCA) offers classes through 8th grade. Our classes are made up of a variety of combination grades and individual grades. Our class sizes are kept small in order to maintain a high level of student and teacher involvement and relationship.

The decision to combine grades is generally determined by enrollment of students in each grade. Planning for each new school year is done as quickly as possible in order to keep parents informed. At CCA we understand that private Christian education is not a decision made lightly by families and it involves many factors.

We welcome visitors to explore our web site and class pages. If you would like additional information about Cornerstone Christian or a campus tour please feel free to contact us.
